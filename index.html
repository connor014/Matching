DAY VS NIGHT

How to play

Having trouble falling asleep? Why count sheep when you can count cards, the aim is simple. Match all the cards and youll get your precious dreams. There is 1 catch. Dont match the game over cards, or its no dreams for you. There is help along the way, match the time cards and youll get additional time, match the lives cards and your lives will increase, match the score cards and your overall score will increase. DO NOT MATCH THE GAME OVER CARDS.
